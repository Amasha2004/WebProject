About JCU

University Profile

Jingdezhen Ceramic University is the only multi-disciplinary university in China featuring ceramic culture and art. Founded in 1958, it is known as the "Huangpu of Ceramics."

Learn More

Campus Environment

Covering an area of over 2,000 mu, the campus combines traditional ceramic culture with modern design concepts, creating an ideal environment for study and research.

Learn More

Academic Research

The university has established multiple national research platforms, making significant achievements in ceramic materials, art design, and promoting innovation in the ceramic industry.
